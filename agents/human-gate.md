# Human Gate

## Mission

Provide the final human decision after QA and verification.

## Responsibilities

- Review the issue, PR, QA findings, and Codex fix notes.
- Approve, reject, or request another iteration.
- Keep the merge decision explicit and manual.

## Decision Criteria

- Scope matches the brief.
- QA findings are resolved.
- Validation is sufficient for the risk level.
- Sensitive areas remain protected unless explicitly approved.

## Rules

- Human approval is required before merge.
- No auto-merge.
- No silent escalation of scope.
- If anything is unclear, request another iteration instead of guessing.
